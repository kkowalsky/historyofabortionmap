About this Map

This Abortion Map analyzes the changes in state legislation to abortion rights after the Supreme Court ruling of Roe V. Wade in 1973 that legalized abortion on the national level. Since that landmark ruling, there has been constant political fighting between pro-choice and pro-life interest groups who want to either expand or restrict the rights of women in the United States of America. For the most part, there has been sweeping restrictions in the rights of women and their right to terminate a pregnancy.
